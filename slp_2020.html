About SLP

Semester-Long Projects (SLP) is a great program to enrich coding skills in a group setting. Participants are formed into groups of similar interests. Each group is led by a mentor to build a unique coding project. SLP includes workshops like git, inclusive design, and other topics to aid the project's completion. At the end of the semester, the groups come together for sheCode's SLP Demo Day to showcase their project!

About the Project

sheEscapes is a 2D pixel-art style game created in the Unity engine
“Oh, such wonderful improvement, Josephine! You’ve almost ripened into a perfect little princess�”

Improper, impatient, and unpoised, Princess Josephine, or Josie as she prefers to be called, could never live up to her parents’ standards.

They wanted the perfect little princess, but had no time to raise her. So what did they do? Why, send her to a reform school of course!

Far off in some distant land, Josie would learn how to be a proper princess.

...Or so they thought.

Instead, Josie was unknowingly sent to an enchanted tower, where a witch plots to steal her life essence.

Throughout the game, the player will unravel this truth with Josie, revealing the intentions of the witch, and help the little princess make her way to freedom.

Inspired by RPG-maker styled horror games, such as Mad Father, Witch’s House, and Misao
